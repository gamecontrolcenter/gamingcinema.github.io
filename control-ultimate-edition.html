Walkthrough Guide

Control Ultimate Edition combines Remedy Entertainment's base game with both the AWE (Alan Wake connection) and Foundation expansions, all running on the updated PlayStation 5 engine for the Ultimate release. Jesse Faden steps into the Oldest House — the Federal Bureau of Control's shifting brutalist headquarters in New York — searching for her brother Dylan and finding herself elevated to Director after the Hiss invasion. The Service Weapon shapeshifts between five forms and pairs with telekinetic abilities for a combat loop built around vertical destruction.

The walkthrough delivers the full Ultimate Edition campaign — base story plus AWE and Foundation — across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. The Janitor Ahti's musical chapter, the Mold-1 confrontation, and the AWE crossover with Alan Wake's narrative are all preserved uncut. Remedy's Brutalist architecture and Petri Alanko's score build an atmosphere that the no-commentary format protects fully.
