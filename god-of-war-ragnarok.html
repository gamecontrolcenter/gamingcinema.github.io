Walkthrough Guide

God of War Ragnarök concludes the Norse saga begun in the 2018 reboot. Kratos and a now-teenaged Atreus traverse all Nine Realms — Midgard, Vanaheim, Asgard, Svartalfheim, Alfheim, Niflheim, Muspelheim, Jötunheim, and Helheim — to confront an Aesir-led prophecy and Odin's ruling order. The combat system inherits the precision of the previous entry while adding the Draupnir Spear, expanded shield mechanics, and a deeper companion roster that includes Tyr, Freya, Mimir, Sindri, and Brok.

Captured across two parts on PlayStation 5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary, the walkthrough preserves every major confrontation: the Thor encounter on the lake, the Heimdall duel, the Odin ravens, and the climactic Ragnarök sequence at Asgard. Quiet moments — Kratos and Atreus' father-and-son exchanges, Freya's mourning, the workshop banter — are preserved at full length so the emotional through-line lands as Santa Monica intended.
