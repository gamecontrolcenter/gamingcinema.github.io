About the Game

God of War Ragnarok concludes Santa Monica Studio's Norse saga, following Kratos and his son Atreus through the prophesied end of the Aesir gods. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling. Combat layers the Leviathan Axe, Blades of Chaos, and the new Draupnir Spear with refined parries and runic attacks. All nine realms are explored in cinematic fidelity, no commentary.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Fimbulwinter

    Three years after 2018's ending, Kratos and Atreus open the game tracking a wolf in deep winter snow before a hostile encounter with Freya.

  2. 2

    Surviving Fimbulwinter

    Sindri and Brok return to repair the workshop; Kratos and Atreus prepare for the journey across realms.

  3. 3

    The Quest for Tyr

    Search for the missing Norse god Tyr to broker an alliance against Odin and prevent the foretold Ragnarok.

  4. 4

    Groa's Shrine

    Vanaheim exploration unlocks the giant Jotunn vision and the prophecy of Atreus's fate as Loki.

  5. 5

    Forging Destiny

    Crafting the Draupnir Spear in Svartalfheim's dwarven forge introduces the new third weapon to the combat sandbox.

  6. 6

    Reunion

    Atreus secretly travels to Asgard alone, befriending Odin and Thrud while Kratos searches Niflheim for the boy.

  7. 7

    The Realms at War

    Open conflict erupts as Kratos rallies Surtr, Freya, and the survivors of Asgard's persecuted dissidents.

  8. 8

    Creatures of Prophecy

    Kratos confronts the prophecy of his own death and accepts the role of leading the assault on Asgard.

  9. 9

    Unleashing Hel

    Garm the monstrous wolf is freed in Helheim, opening rifts across all nine realms and accelerating Ragnarok.

  10. 10

    The Summoning

    Allied armies gather at the Shores of Nine — dwarves, Vanir, Asgardian rebels, and giants prepare to march.

  11. 11

    Ragnarok

    The full-scale assault on Asgard with Thor boss fight, the destruction of the Bifrost, and the city's collapse.

  12. 12

    Boss: Odin

    Final confrontation with the Allfather in his transformed magical form atop the broken throne room ruins.

  13. 13

    Ending: Father and Son

    Kratos accepts a new role as a hero remembered by the realms; Atreus departs to find other giants in hiding.
