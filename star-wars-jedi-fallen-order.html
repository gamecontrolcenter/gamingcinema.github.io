About the Game

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order is Respawn Entertainment's 2019 Soulslike action-adventure set in the dark times after Order 66. Padawan survivor Cal Kestis attempts to rebuild the Jedi Order while hunted by Imperial Inquisitors. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. The cinematic playthrough emphasizes the Metroidvania-style world design.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Bracca Scrapyard

    Opening Imperial scrap planet level establishing Cal's lightsaber discovery and his initial escape from the Empire.

  2. 2

    Bogano Sanctuary

    First major Metroidvania-style planet introducing the Mantis crew (Cere, Greez, BD-1) and the abandoned vault below.

  3. 3

    Zeffo: The Tomb of Eilram

    First Force tomb dungeon unlocking the Force Pull ability and exploring the Zeffo's ancient civilization.

  4. 4

    Dathomir Trial

    Optional but recommended early visit to Dathomir's Nightbrother brotherhood introducing Merrin and double-bladed lightsaber.

  5. 5

    Kashyyyk Rescue

    Wookiee homeworld rescue mission liberating Wyyyschokk capture and introducing Saw Gerrera's Rebel partisans.

  6. 6

    Boss: Ninth Sister

    First major Imperial Inquisitor boss fight in Kashyyyk's forest canopy testing the established combat fundamentals.

  7. 7

    Zeffo: The Tomb of Miktrull

    Second Force tomb unlocking Force Push and revealing more of the Zeffo people's connection to the Force itself.

  8. 8

    Boss: Second Sister

    Confrontation with the masked Inquisitor — Trilla Suduri, former Jedi Padawan now turned Imperial enforcer.

  9. 9

    Ilum Crystal Quest

    Pilgrimage to the Jedi crystal cave on Ilum following the climactic Force vision that re-tempers Cal's blade.

  10. 10

    Boss: Trilla Final Duel

    Climactic Inquisitor duel inside the Imperial Star Destroyer's interrogation chamber with cinematic phases.

  11. 11

    Boss: Darth Vader

    Penultimate forced-survival encounter against Vader himself in the Fortress Inquisitorius water-filled emergency escape.

  12. 12

    Ending: Jedi Holocron Destroyed

    Cal makes the morally complex choice to destroy the holocron rather than risk the Empire hunting Force-sensitive children.
