Walkthrough Guide

Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order introduces Cal Kestis, a Padawan who survived Order 66 by hiding on the scrapyard planet Bracca. When his abilities are exposed, he joins former Jedi Cere Junda and pilot Greez Dritus aboard the Mantis to find a Holocron containing the names of Force-sensitive children before the Inquisitorius reaches it. The Souls-like combat, Metroidvania traversal, and lightsaber stance progression define a game that successfully repositioned Star Wars within single-player action gaming.

The complete walkthrough is captured across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. Every planet — Bogano, Zeffo, Kashyyyk, Dathomir, Ilum, Nur — is traversed in full, with the named confrontations including the Second Sister, the Ninth Sister, and the surprise Darth Vader sequence preserved at cinematic length. BD-1's chirps, Gordy Haab's score, and the Star Wars Legends-flavored lore carry the experience uninterrupted.
