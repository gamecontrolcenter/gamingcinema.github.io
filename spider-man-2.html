About the Game

Marvel's Spider-Man 2 from Insomniac Games is the PS5 sequel pairing Peter Parker and Miles Morales as dual playable heroes in an expanded Marvel's New York featuring Queens and Brooklyn. Kraven the Hunter arrives in the city hunting metahumans while Peter struggles with the alien symbiote suit. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.
