About the Game

Indiana Jones and the Great Circle is MachineGames' first-person action-adventure following Dr. Henry Jones Jr. in 1937, investigating a global conspiracy after a midnight artifact theft at Marshall College. The walkthrough captures the full campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. The cinematic playthrough showcases the game's photoreal facial capture of Troy Baker as Indy and the period-accurate art direction.

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

    Prologue: The Marshall College Midnight Break-in

    Stealth-focused opening chapter establishing Indy's whip combat, fist-fighting, and the artifact theft that drives the plot.
