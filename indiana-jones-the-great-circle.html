Walkthrough Guide

Indiana Jones and the Great Circle is MachineGames' first-person action-adventure starring the legendary archaeologist between Raiders of the Lost Ark and The Last Crusade. The campaign globetrots from Marshall College and the Vatican Archives in Rome to the Sukhothai temples of Thailand, the Giza Plateau in Egypt, and a Himalayan monastery, chasing the mystery of the Great Circle alongside ally Gina Lombardi while opposed by Emmerich Voss.

The complete two-part walkthrough is captured on PlayStation 5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. Part I covers the opening break-in, Vatican infiltration, Roman rooftop chase, and the Sukhothai chapter; Part II takes the story through the Pyramid trials, Nazi dig site, dogfight escape, Himalayan ascent, and the final boss confrontation with Voss inside the Great Circle. Whip combat, signature melee brawls, and the original Indiana Jones theme score are all preserved at cinematic length.
