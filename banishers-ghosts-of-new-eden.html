About the Game

Banishers: Ghosts of New Eden is Don't Nod's action RPG set in 17th century colonial New England. Red and Antea are Banishers — supernatural hunters tasked with exorcising the dead. After a tragic incident at the haunted village of New Eden, Antea becomes a ghost herself, and Red must choose between resurrecting her at terrible cost or letting her ascend. The walkthrough captures the full campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Arrival in New Eden

    Prologue introducing Red and Antea as banisher partners arriving at the cursed colonial village investigation request.

  2. 2

    The Nightmare

    First major encounter with the village's haunting force, ending in Antea's death and her transformation into a ghost.

  3. 3

    The Quest for Resolution

    Red awakens to the moral choice driving the campaign: ascension (peace) or resurrection (revenge). The first major village hub opens.

  4. 4

    Coastal Wilds

    Open-area exploration of New Eden's foggy coastal forests, introducing the haunting case-investigation gameplay loop.

  5. 5

    The Lighthouse Spirit

    First major haunting case at the abandoned lighthouse keeper's residence with branching morality choices.

  6. 6

    Trapper's Sorrow

    Side haunting case in the fur trapper's cabin testing the player's chosen ascension-versus-blame philosophy.

  7. 7

    The Settler Encampment

    Mid-game hub town with the densest side quest content and the most varied haunting case morality outcomes.

  8. 8

    The Iron Mine

    Industrial dungeon level beneath an abandoned mine with the most cumulative haunting from murdered miners.

  9. 9

    The Witch's Cabin

    Eponymous haunting at the village witch's burned cabin reveals the deeper conspiracy behind New Eden's curse.

  10. 10

    The Final Investigation

    Climactic case uncovering the founding settler's atrocity that triggered the curse cascading across New Eden.

  11. 11

    Ending: Ascension or Resurrection

    Two divergent endings determined by Red's accumulated choices — Antea ascends to rest or is bound to Red's body.
