About the Game

Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales is the standalone Insomniac follow-up to the 2018 original, following Miles Morales' first solo Spider-Man Christmas in a snow-covered Harlem. Peter Parker is away in Europe so Miles takes up the mantle alone against the Tinkerer and the Roxxon Energy corporation. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.
