Walkthrough Guide

Resident Evil 4 Remake reimagines Capcom's 2005 masterpiece with modern combat, restructured pacing, and the visual fidelity demanded by the PlayStation 5 Pro. Six years after the events of Raccoon City, Leon S. Kennedy travels to a remote European village on a presidential rescue mission for Ashley Graham. The walkthrough covers the entire campaign across two parts — village, castle, and island — and includes every Ganados encounter, every merchant interaction, and the full slate of boss fights against Bitores Mendez, Ramon Salazar, Jack Krauser, and Lord Saddler.

Captured at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, the no-commentary presentation lets Capcom's atmospheric sound design lead. Las Plagas reveals, Regenerator confrontations, and the central Ashley sequences are preserved at full cinematic length. Part I covers the village and early castle chapters; Part II completes the castle siege, the island assault, and the final confrontation with Saddler.
