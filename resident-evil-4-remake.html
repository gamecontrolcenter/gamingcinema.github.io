About the Game

Resident Evil 4 Remake reimagines Capcom's 2005 classic with the RE Engine, rebuilt combat systems, and modernized pacing. Leon S. Kennedy travels to a rural European village to rescue the President's daughter Ashley Graham from a parasitic cult known as Los Iluminados. The walkthrough captures the full campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling — Village, Castle, and Island acts rendered with high-fidelity lighting and crisp particle effects. No commentary, pure cinematic playthrough.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Village Outskirts

    Leon's arrival in rural Spain, first Ganado encounters, and the iconic chainsaw villager fight in the opening farm sequence.

  2. 2

    Village Square

    Massive set-piece defense against waves of villagers, culminating with the church bell signal that ends the assault.

  3. 3

    Pueblo Outskirts

    Tracking Ashley through the surrounding woods, mining tunnels, and the first encounter with Del Lago in the lake boss fight.

  4. 4

    Castle Approach

    Transition into the gothic Salazar Castle territory, introducing armored knights, mendez encounters, and merchant exchanges.

  5. 5

    Castle Interior

    Ashley accompanies Leon through ornate halls, puzzle rooms, and the harrowing Garrador blind enemy encounters.

  6. 6

    Sewers and Cells

    Castle dungeon descent with regenerating verdugo enemies and the iconic minecart escape sequence.

  7. 7

    Salazar Confrontation

    Two-phase boss fight against Ramon Salazar and his transformed final form deep within the castle keep.

  8. 8

    Island Approach

    Boat infiltration of the military island base where Saddler runs the cult's command operations.

  9. 9

    Island Facility

    Heavily armed Ganados patrol modern industrial areas; gunpowder Ada Wong subplot intersects the main path.

  10. 10

    Krauser Duel

    Knife-on-knife dueling sequence against Leon's former Operation Javier ally turned villain.

  11. 11

    Final Boss: Saddler

    Multi-stage cult leader boss combining tentacled mutation phases and the rocket launcher finale on the helipad.

  12. 12

    Escape

    Time-limited jet-ski escape sequence with Ashley as the island self-destructs, closing the campaign.
