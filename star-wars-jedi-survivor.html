About the Game

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor is Respawn's sequel to Fallen Order, set five years after the original. Cal Kestis is now a battle-hardened Jedi fighting the Empire as it tightens its grip on the galaxy. New abilities include dual-stance lightsaber styles (Single, Double-Bladed, Dual Wield, Blaster, Crossguard). The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.
