About the Game

Marvel's Spider-Man Remastered is the PS5 enhanced edition of the 2018 original, with rebuilt facial capture, improved lighting, and ray-traced reflections across Manhattan. Peter Parker has been Spider-Man for eight years and balances superheroics with his day job at Otto Octavius's lab. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.
