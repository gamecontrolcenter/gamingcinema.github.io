Walkthrough Guide

Marvel's Spider-Man Remastered is Insomniac Games' PlayStation 5 reissue of the 2018 original, with updated character models, ray-traced reflections, and a redesigned Peter Parker face model. The story follows a more experienced Peter Parker eight years into his Spider-Man career, working alongside Mary Jane Watson, Aunt May, Otto Octavius, and Yuri Watanabe as Mister Negative's Demons, the Sinister Six, and a city-shaking finale unfold across Manhattan.

The walkthrough captures the full main story across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. Every named villain encounter — Kingpin, Shocker, Vulture, Electro, Rhino, Scorpion, Mister Negative, and Doctor Octopus — and every Mary Jane and Miles stealth chapter is preserved at cinematic length. John Paesano's score, the iconic web-swing soundscape, and the original voice work carry uninterrupted.
