Walkthrough Guide

Horizon Zero Dawn Remastered is Guerrilla Games' modernized rebuild of the 2017 original, using Horizon Forbidden West's engine to update the post-apocalyptic adventure of Aloy through Nora, Carja, and Banuk territories. The narrative of Aloy's hunt for the truth behind her birth, the Faro Plague, and Project Zero Dawn remains untouched, while the visual and animation upgrades bring the original cast — Rost, Erend, Varl, Sylens, Ted Faro — closer to the Forbidden West fidelity standard.

The walkthrough runs across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. Every Cauldron override, every Tallneck climb, and every main story beat through the Meridian siege and the HADES confrontation is preserved. The Thunderjaw and Stormbird encounters that defined the original's combat identity are captured at full cinematic length, with the Joris de Man / Niels van der Leest score delivering the legendary Horizon atmospheric design uninterrupted.
