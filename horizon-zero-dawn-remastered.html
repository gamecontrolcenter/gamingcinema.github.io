About the Game

Horizon Zero Dawn Remastered is Guerrilla Games' enhanced edition of the 2017 original, with PS5 Pro native rendering, rebuilt environmental detail, and reworked facial capture. Aloy, a Nora outcast, sets out to discover her origins and the mystery behind the giant machines that roam the post-apocalyptic Earth. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary, including The Frozen Wilds DLC.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Prologue: A Present from the Past

    Young Aloy discovers the Focus device in the Nora caves, beginning her journey as Rost's outcast pupil.

  2. 2

    The Proving

    The Nora tribe ceremony where Aloy competes for tribal recognition before the catastrophic attack by Eclipse zealots.

  3. 3

    The Womb of the Mountain

    Investigation of the All-Mother sacred caves revealing the first hints of Aloy's true origin and the Old Ones tech.

  4. 4

    The City of the Sun

    Arrival in Meridian, the Carja capital city, with diplomatic intrigue and the Sun King Avad's court.

  5. 5

    The Field of the Fallen

    Battle-scarred plains exploration with the first major Cauldron infiltration unlocking machine overrides.

  6. 6

    Old World Faro Automated Solutions corporate ruins reveal the truth of the Faro Plague and humanity's extinction.

  7. 7

    The Grave Hoard

  8. 8

    GAIA Prime

  9. 9

    The Battle of Meridian

    Penultimate large-scale battle defending the Carja capital from the corrupted HADES forces and Eclipse cult.

  10. 10

    Final confrontation against the corrupted extinction AI HADES atop the Spire in central Meridian.

  11. 11

    The Frozen Wilds DLC

    Post-game expansion in the snowy Cut region featuring the daemonic CYAN AI and the new fire-machine variants.
