Walkthrough Guide

Hogwarts Legacy places the player as a fifth-year transfer student to Hogwarts in the late 1800s, decades before the Harry Potter timeline, with an Ancient Magic plot threading the campaign together. The open-world Wizarding World includes Hogsmeade Village, the Forbidden Forest, and a region-spanning map dotted with field guide pages, Merlin Trials, and Bandit Camps. Players select house — Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, or Hufflepuff — and learn dozens of spells across combat, exploration, and stealth.

The complete walkthrough spans three parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. The main story arc — Professor Fig, Professor Black, Sebastian Sallow, Ominis Gaunt, Natty, Poppy — is preserved through to the Ranrok confrontation and the Keepers' final reveal. Every major story beat, including the Unforgivable Curses arc through Sebastian's relationship questline, lands at full cinematic pace alongside the John Williams–influenced original score.
