About the Game

Hogwarts Legacy is Avalanche Software's open-world action RPG set in the late 1800s wizarding world, a century before the Harry Potter book series. The player creates a fifth-year transfer student to Hogwarts who possesses rare Ancient Magic, drawn into a goblin rebellion led by Ranrok. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across three parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.
