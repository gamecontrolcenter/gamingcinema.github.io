Walkthrough Guide

Stellar Blade is Shift Up's PlayStation 5 exclusive that put the Korean studio on the mainstream gaming map. Players control Eve, an airborne paratrooper sent to retake an Earth overrun by hostile Naytiba, with Adam and Lily forming her ground support in the ruined city of Eidos 7. The combat system blends parry-focused melee, Beta Skill super attacks, and Burst abilities into a flow closer to Bayonetta than to Souls-likes, while exploration takes Eve through wastelands, abandoned subways, and lush vertical environments.

The walkthrough delivers the full campaign across two parts plus a dedicated Boss Fights compilation, all captured on PlayStation 5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. Every named enemy — from Maelstrom and Gigas to Stalker, Abaddon, and the late-game encounter with Tachy — is preserved, and Eve's costume changes and skill tree progression are uninterrupted so the visual and mechanical layers both land at full clarity.
