About the Game

Stellar Blade is Shift Up's action title following EVE, a member of the 7th Airborne Squad sent from a space colony to reclaim Earth from the Naytibas, mutated creatures who drove humanity off the planet. The fast, precision-based combat draws comparison to Sekiro and NieR, with parries, dodges, and Beta/Burst skill trees. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across three parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Eidos 7 Drop

    Tutorial drop zone establishing combat fundamentals and EVE's connection to her squadron leader Tachy in the ruined city.

  2. 2

    Wasteland Camp

    First semi-open hub area introducing the survivor Adam, the merchant Lily, and the side mission structure.

  3. 3

    Altess Levoire

    Major dungeon-style location with vertical platforming, the iconic Tachy duel, and the first major boss encounter.

  4. 4

    Eidos 7 Underground

    Subterranean exploration unlocks the second city zone with rope-grapple traversal and the Brute Naytiba miniboss.

    Sci-fi facility level with environmental puzzles, the truth about EVE's nature, and elite enemy ambush rooms.

  6. 6

    Spire 4

  7. 7

    Boss: Stalker

    Mid-game rival fight introducing the parry-perfect dodge system at maximum intensity in the city ruins.

    Open semi-arid zone with sand-buggy traversal, faction encounters, and the largest side-quest density.

  9. 9

  10. 10

    Boss: Raven

  11. 11

  12. 12

    Ending: New Earth
