About the Game

Resident Evil 2 Remake is Capcom's 2019 reimagining of the 1998 PlayStation classic, rebuilt in the RE Engine with over-the-shoulder controls and reactive zombie AI. Rookie cop Leon Kennedy and college student Claire Redfield navigate the doomed Raccoon City Police Department on his first day on the job, the same night the T-Virus outbreak transforms the city. This walkthrough captures the full playthrough on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary, focused on cinematic storytelling.
