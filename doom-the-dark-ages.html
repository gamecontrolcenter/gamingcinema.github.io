Walkthrough Guide

Doom: The Dark Ages is id Software's medieval-fantasy prequel to the modern Doom trilogy, recasting the Slayer as a sword-and-shield warrior fighting through Argent D'Nur and across hellish battlefields. The Shield Saw replaces traditional dodge mechanics — players parry, block, and counterattack in a grounded combat loop closer to a heavy-metal Dark Souls than the airborne movement of Doom Eternal. Mounted dragon flight and the colossal Atlan mech segments break the rhythm with set-piece spectacle.

The walkthrough hits every chapter, every boss confrontation, and every weapon unlock — from the Skullcrusher to the BFG's medieval variant. id Software's Mick Gordon–style soundtrack and ambient demon roars carry uninterrupted, making the entire campaign play like a heavy-metal feature film on the big screen.
