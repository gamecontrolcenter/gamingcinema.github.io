About the Game

Silent Hill 2 Remake is Bloober Team and Konami's full ground-up remake of the 2001 PlayStation 2 psychological horror classic. James Sunderland returns to the cursed town of Silent Hill after receiving a letter from his late wife Mary. The walkthrough captures the full campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. The cinematic playthrough preserves the slow, dread-filled pacing of the original.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Toluca Lake

    Prologue at the lake's foggy shore where James reads the letter from his deceased wife Mary calling him to Silent Hill.

  2. 2

    Wood Side Apartments

    First major dungeon in the abandoned Wood Side complex introducing Pyramid Head and the closet locked Mannequin enemy.

  3. 3

    Blue Creek Apartments

    Second apartment block exploration with the iconic mirror room and the encounter with Angela Orosco.

  4. 4

    Brookhaven Hospital

    Mid-game psychiatric hospital sequence with the Bubble Head Nurse enemies and the disturbing Eddie cafeteria encounter.

  5. 5

    Otherworld Hospital

    Transition to the Otherworld version of Brookhaven with the dreadful long-corridor pursuit sequence.

  6. 6

    Toluca Prison

    Underground prison level with the second Pyramid Head encounter and the discovery of James' true nature.

  7. 7

    Lakeview Hotel

    Penultimate location at the burned hotel where James and Mary spent their honeymoon, revealing the campaign's truth.

  8. 8

    Otherworld Lakeview

    Climactic descent through the corrupted version of the hotel with the dual Pyramid Head boss fight.

  9. 9

    Ending: Truth

    Cinematic finale with multiple possible endings determined by accumulated player behavior throughout the campaign.
