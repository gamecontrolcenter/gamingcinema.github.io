Walkthrough Guide

Ghost of Tsushima is Sucker Punch Productions' 2020 samurai open-world adventure set during the first Mongol invasion of Japan in 1274. Jin Sakai, last of his clan after the Mongol landing at Komoda Beach, sets out to liberate Tsushima Island from Khotun Khan, allying with Yuna, Sensei Ishikawa, Lady Masako, Norio, and the assassin Tomoe while gradually abandoning the samurai code for the more pragmatic 'Ghost' identity.

The complete walkthrough runs across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. The main story — Komoda Beach, Castle Kaneda, Mongol fortress assaults, Lord Shimura confrontation — is preserved at cinematic length. Sucker Punch's wind-guided navigation, the Director's Cut Iki Island expansion (where applicable), the Mythic Tales side quests, and Shigeru Umebayashi's score all carry uninterrupted, making this one of the channel's most visually iconic open-world walkthroughs.
