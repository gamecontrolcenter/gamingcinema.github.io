About the Game

Ghost of Tsushima is Sucker Punch Productions' open-world samurai epic set in 1274 during the first Mongol invasion of Japan. Jin Sakai, the last of his clan, must abandon the samurai code to wage guerrilla war against the invaders as the legendary Ghost. The walkthrough captures the full campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary, including the Iki Island expansion content.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Komoda Beach

    Opening battle disaster on Komoda Beach where the samurai forces fall to Mongol khan Khotun Khan in the first invasion wave.

  2. 2

    Yuna's Aid

    Thief Yuna rescues a wounded Jin and treats his injuries, beginning the journey to rescue his uncle Lord Shimura.

  3. 3

    Castle Kaneda

    First major rescue operation infiltrating the Mongol-held castle to free Jin's uncle from the Khan's capture.

  4. 4

    Ryuzo Betrayal

    Jin's childhood friend Ryuzo turns traitor as his Straw Hat ronin band sides with the Mongols for survival.

  5. 5

    The Tale of Sensei Ishikawa

    First major companion side-arc following the master archer hunting his former student turned traitor.

  6. 6

    The Tale of Lady Masako

    Female samurai companion arc seeking revenge against the conspirators who massacred her family.

  7. 7

    The Six Blades of Kojiro

    Mythic Tales side mission introducing the dual-wielding ronin assassin and dueling combat challenges.

  8. 8

    Toyotama Region

    Mid-game opening of the second island region with new Mongol commanders and stealth-heavy operations.

  9. 9

    Mongol Stronghold Liberation

    Series of major stronghold infiltrations using the Ghost stance and chain-assassination mechanics.

  10. 10

    Kamiagata Region

    Final region of the main island with the coldest terrain, hardest enemy density, and most dramatic vistas.

  11. 11

    Boss: Khotun Khan

    Multi-phase climactic duel against the Khan in a burning fortress with environmental fire hazards and minions.

  12. 12

    Iki Island Expansion

    Post-game DLC arc following Jin to Iki Island confronting his past trauma and the Eagle Mongol shamans.
