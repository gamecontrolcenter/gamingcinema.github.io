Walkthrough Guide

Echoes of the End is an Icelandic action-adventure from Myrkur Games following Ryn, a former soldier whose latent magical abilities draw her into a continent-spanning conspiracy alongside companion Abram. The combat blends sword strikes with elemental abilities — telekinesis, ranged blasts, environmental manipulation — and the world design leans heavily on Icelandic landscape inspiration, with towering volcanic ridges and frost-bitten coastlines forming the backdrop to story chapters.
