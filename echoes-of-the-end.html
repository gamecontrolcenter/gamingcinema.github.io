About the Game

Echoes of the End is an Icelandic action-adventure from Myrkur Games following Ryn, a former soldier whose latent magical abilities draw her into a continent-spanning conspiracy alongside companion Abram. Combat blends sword strikes with elemental abilities — telekinesis, ranged blasts, environmental manipulation. The world design draws heavily on Icelandic landscape inspiration, with volcanic ridges and frost-bitten coastlines as the backdrop. The walkthrough captures the full game across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Awakening

    Prologue introduces Ryn's amnesia and her first manifestation of magical abilities during a hostile encounter.

  2. 2

    The Forest Path

    Open-area exploration teaches movement, basic sword combat, and the initial telekinesis ability tree.

  3. 3

    Ruins of the Old Empire

    First dungeon-style location with environmental puzzles, ancient lore, and the first major creature encounter.

  4. 4

    Abram's Camp

    Companion introduction; the deuteragonist joins Ryn's journey and introduces the dual-character combat dynamic.

    Volcanic Highlands

    Mid-game traversal across active volcanic terrain with new heat-based enemy types and platforming challenges.

    The Frozen Sea

  7. 7

    Boss: The Echo

    First major boss fight against a shadow manifestation of Ryn's past, testing all unlocked abilities so far.

  8. 8

    The Capital

    Urban hub area with NPC interactions, political intrigue, and side-quest density in the largest open zone.

  9. 9

    Underground Archive

    Lore-heavy dungeon revealing the true conspiracy behind Ryn's powers and the kingdom's history.

  10. 10

    Final Confrontation

    Multi-phase climax against the antagonist combining all elemental abilities and Abram's combat assistance.

  11. 11

    Ending: The Echo Resolved

    Cinematic resolution determining Ryn's choice between erasing her powers or accepting the responsibility.
