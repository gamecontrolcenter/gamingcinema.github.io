Walkthrough Guide

Alan Wake 2 is Remedy Entertainment's long-awaited return to Bright Falls, Washington, thirteen in-game years after Alan's disappearance into the Dark Place. The story alternates between two playable protagonists — FBI agent Saga Anderson, investigating ritualistic murders in the Pacific Northwest, and Alan Wake himself, trapped inside a shifting noir-Manhattan Dark Place where the manuscript he writes warps reality. The Mind Place and the Writer's Room mechanics build narrative puzzles directly into the gameplay loop.

Captured across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary, the walkthrough preserves the full arc — Saga's investigation through Cauldron Lake and Watery, Alan's traversal of the Dark Place subway and lighthouse, the Old Gods of Asgard musical chapter, and the climactic Lake House finale. The Remedy Connected Universe references to Control are fully captured so the cross-over context lands.
