About the Game

Alan Wake 2 is Remedy Entertainment's long-awaited sequel to the 2010 cult horror title, switching between FBI Agent Saga Anderson investigating a ritual murder in Bright Falls and novelist Alan Wake trapped in the Dark Place. The walkthrough captures the full game across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. Remedy's signature live-action FMV cutscenes intercut with the cinematic gameplay.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Return 1: Invitation

    Saga Anderson and partner Alex Casey arrive at Cauldron Lake investigating the ritualistic Cult of the Tree murder.

  2. 2

    Return 2: The Heart

    Mind Place mechanic is introduced; Saga assembles her case board to deduce locations from collected evidence.

  3. 3

    Return 3: Local Girl

    Investigation in downtown Bright Falls turning up the Anderson Brothers connection and the Old Gods of Asgard band.

  4. 4

    Initiation 1: Late Night

    Switch to Alan Wake trapped in the Dark Place 13 years later, learning the Writer's Room mechanic to alter reality.

  5. 5

    Initiation 2: Casey

    Alan rewrites the city around himself, shifting between subway stations and apartment buildings in noir New York.

  6. 6

    Return 4: Old Gods

    Saga's investigation at Watery introduces the rocker brothers and their lost album holding clues to the cult.

  7. 7

    Initiation 3: Dark Ones

    Alan progresses through the Dark Place's manuscript pages, recasting events around the Cult of the Tree's origin.

  8. 8

    Return 5: Old Gods of Asgard

    Live-action musical sequence performed by Poets of the Fall as the Old Gods of Asgard band — Remedy signature moment.

  9. 9

    Return 6: Zane's Film

    Saga uncovers filmmaker Thomas Zane's connection to the events of the original Alan Wake; the loop deepens.

  10. 10

    Initiation 7: Masters

    Alan confronts Scratch, his dark doppelganger; merging gameplay and narrative threads from both protagonists.

  11. 11

    Ending: Departure

    Climactic dual-protagonist finale at Cauldron Lake; the loop is broken but the Dark Place remains.
