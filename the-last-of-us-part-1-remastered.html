Walkthrough Guide

The Last of Us Part I is Naughty Dog's PlayStation 5 ground-up rebuild of the 2013 original, using the Part II engine to bring updated character models, improved AI behaviors, and modernized accessibility options to the foundational story of Joel and Ellie. Twenty years after the Cordyceps outbreak collapses civilization, smuggler Joel agrees to escort fourteen-year-old Ellie across the United States to the Fireflies, encountering Tess, Bill, Henry, Sam, David, and Marlene along the way.

The complete walkthrough is captured across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. Every story sequence — the Boston QZ, Pittsburgh detour, Lakeside Resort winter chapter, and the Salt Lake City finale — is preserved at full length. Gustavo Santaolalla's iconic guitar score and the original voice performances from Troy Baker and Ashley Johnson are the centerpiece of the cinematic experience.
