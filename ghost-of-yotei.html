Walkthrough Guide

Ghost of Yōtei is Sucker Punch Productions' follow-up to Ghost of Tsushima, relocating the open-world samurai formula to Hokkaidō's Ezo region. Players take on Atsu, an onryō-driven warrior hunting the Six of Yōtei across a Mount Yōtei landscape that draws heavily on Ainu cultural reference and period-accurate Edo-adjacent atmospheres. Stance-based katana combat, mounted traversal, and standalone duels against named enemies — the Snake, the Spider, the Dragon, the Oni, the Kitsune, and the lead villain — structure the campaign.

The complete two-part walkthrough is captured on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling and no commentary. Every named duel is preserved at cinematic length, including the climactic confrontation at Mount Yōtei itself. The pillar-of-fire ambient design and Ilan Eshkeri's score carry uninterrupted, and Sucker Punch's signature wind-direction navigation hint stays in frame so the world's aesthetic philosophy remains intact for the viewer.
