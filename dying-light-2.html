About the Game

Dying Light 2: Stay Human is Techland's open-world parkour-zombie sequel set 15 years after the original. Aiden Caldwell, a Pilgrim, arrives in the City — one of the last human settlements — searching for his missing sister. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign across two parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. Both day and night gameplay loops are preserved with their distinct combat tones.
