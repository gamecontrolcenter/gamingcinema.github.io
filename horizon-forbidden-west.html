Walkthrough Guide

Horizon Forbidden West continues Aloy's quest to restore Earth's biosphere after the events of Zero Dawn, travelling west from Carja territory into the Tenakth-dominated western lands. New tribes, the Quen newcomers from beyond the seas, and a returning antagonist arc featuring Sylens drive a continent-spanning narrative across Vegas ruins, San Francisco bay, and Yosemite analogues. Aloy gains the Pullcaster, the Shieldwing, and the Diving Mask, opening verticality, glide traversal, and underwater chapters absent from the original.

The complete walkthrough runs across three parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary. Every main quest, every Tallneck override, and every Cauldron is preserved. Boss encounters — Tremortusk, Slitherfangs, Slaughterspine — and the climactic confrontation against the antagonist faction are all captured at cinematic length, with Guerrilla's environmental storytelling and Joris de Man's expanded score delivering the experience uninterrupted.
