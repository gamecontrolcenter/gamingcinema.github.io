About the Game

Horizon Forbidden West is Guerrilla Games' sequel set six months after Zero Dawn. Aloy travels west across post-apocalyptic North America (modern California, Nevada, and Utah) to find the source of a deadly red blight killing biospheres and confront the Far Zenith rogue AI faction. The walkthrough captures the full campaign including the Burning Shores DLC across three parts on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    Reach for the Stars

    Opening prologue establishes the blight crisis and Aloy's quest west, ending with the search for GAIA's backup.

  2. 2

    To the Brink

    Crossing the Forbidden West frontier into Tenakth tribal territory; first encounters with new Clamberjaw machines.

  3. 3

    The Embassy

    Major hub town introducing diplomatic gameplay between Tenakth clans, the Carja envoys, and Aloy's mediator role.

  4. 4

    The Daunt

    First major exploration zone with new Slitherfang machines and the workshop-restoration quest line.

  5. 5

    Death's Door

    Discovery of the Zenith faction's true origins and their pursuit of GAIA's subordinate functions across the west.

  6. 6

    Faro's Tomb

    Dungeon descent into Ted Faro's hidden bunker revealing his transformed mutated form in horror-tinged sequences.

  7. 7

    The Sea of Sands

    Open desert traversal with the first appearance of the colossal Tremortusk machines and dune-buggy stealth.

  8. 8

    Cauldron IOTA

    Mid-game machine factory infiltration unlocking the ability to override Tremortusks and Apex predator machines.

  9. 9

    Boss: Erend's Rescue

    Major mid-game set piece rescuing Aloy's ally with Tremortusk warthog combat and the Tideripper boss.

  10. 10

    Singularity

    Penultimate chapter assaulting the Far Zenith base in Nevada with the highest enemy density in the campaign.

  11. 11

    Boss: Tilda van der Meer

    Final confrontation against the immortal Zenith antagonist piloting a Specter combat mech in a mountaintop arena.

  12. 12

    Burning Shores DLC

    Post-game expansion taking Aloy to Los Angeles' flooded ruins, featuring the colossal Horus Titan boss climax.
