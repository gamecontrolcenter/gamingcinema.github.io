About the Game

Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart is Insomniac Games' PS5 launch-window title showcasing the SSD with instantaneous dimensional rift transitions. Ratchet and Clank confront Dr. Nefarious in a multiverse-spanning adventure that introduces Rivet, a Lombax resistance fighter from an alternate dimension.
