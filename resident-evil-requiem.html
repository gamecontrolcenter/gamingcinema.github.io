Resident Evil Requiem is Capcom's latest entry in the long-running Resident Evil franchise, picking up the series' next chapter with new protagonist Grace Ashcroft. The story unfolds inside the sprawling Ashcroft Estate and the laboratory complex beneath it, where decades of corporate experiments have left behind a contained outbreak and a singular antagonist — the Requiem Entity. Players navigate detailed Gothic interiors, sunken gardens, and an observatory tower while solving mechanical puzzles, managing limited ammunition, and confronting bosses such as the Corrupted Surgeon and the Mutated Guardian.
