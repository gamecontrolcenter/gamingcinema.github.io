Resident Evil 3 Remake retells Jill Valentine's desperate escape from Raccoon City as Umbrella's Nemesis bio-weapon hunts her relentlessly through the collapsing streets. Alongside UBCS operative Carlos Oliveira, the campaign moves from downtown rooftops to the police station, the city hospital, and the underground NEST 2 facility. The faster pacing and dodge-focused combat differentiate it from RE2 Remake while preserving the same RE Engine production values.
