Walkthrough Guide

God of War (2018) restarts the franchise in Norse mythology, introducing a weathered older Kratos and his son Atreus as they journey to scatter the ashes of Faye from the highest peak in the realms. The reinvented Leviathan Axe combat, single-shot camera, and intimate father-son narrative redefined what God of War could be after the Greek-era originals. Boss encounters with Baldur, the Stranger, set against the looming presence of Jörmungandr the World Serpent, anchor the campaign.

The complete walkthrough is captured at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling on PlayStation 5 Pro, no commentary. Every realm transition — Midgard, Alfheim, Helheim, Jötunheim — and every Valkyrie-adjacent set piece is preserved. The intentionally quiet stretches of the journey, where the dialogue between Kratos and Atreus carries the story, remain uncut so the relationship development reads at the pace Santa Monica intended.
