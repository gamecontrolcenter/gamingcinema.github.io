About the Game

God of War (2018) reboots Santa Monica Studio's franchise with a one-take camera, Norse mythology setting, and an emphasis on father-son storytelling. Kratos and his young son Atreus journey across Midgard, Alfheim, and Helheim to scatter the ashes of Kratos's wife from the highest peak in the realms. The walkthrough captures the full main campaign on PS5 Pro at 4K HDR 60FPS with PSSR upscaling, no commentary, presented as one continuous cinematic playthrough.

Walkthrough — Chapter Breakdown

A complete chapter-by-chapter overview of the campaign. Use this guide to track story progress, locate specific moments in the videos above, or plan a multi-session viewing.

  1. 1

    The Marked Trees

    Cold open in the woodland home where Kratos teaches Atreus to hunt; the Stranger arrives setting the conflict in motion.

  2. 2

    Path to the Mountain

    First journey toward the highest peak begins; mystic gateway puzzles introduce the Bifrost mechanic.

  3. 3

    The Light of Alfheim

    Realm-hopping to recover the bridge keeper light from the dark elves and Light Elves' civil war.

  4. 4

    Inside the Mountain

    Mining tunnels lead deep into Midgard's heart with the first Hel-Walker undead encounters.

  5. 5

    A New Destination

    Pivotal twist redirects Kratos and Atreus toward Jotunheim, the giants' realm, rather than the original peak.

  6. 6

    The Sickness

    Atreus falls gravely ill forcing a desperate descent into Helheim to retrieve the heart of a Helbringer.

  7. 7

    The Magic Chisel

    Tyr's temple puzzle gauntlet at the World Serpent's spine, recovering the chisel needed to unseal Jotunheim.

  8. 8

    Behind the Lock

    Black Rune restoration in Veithurgard area, opening the secret passage to the giants' lost knowledge.

  9. 9

    The Black Rune

    Combat-heavy chapter through Konunsgard's ruins, retrieving the Black Rune from the dragon Hraezlyr.

  10. 10

    Boss: Baldur

    Multiple-phase final boss against the immortal Stranger atop the world serpent's body in the climactic battle.

  11. 11

    Mother's Ashes

    Ending pilgrimage to the Jotunheim peak; Faye's true heritage as a giant is revealed via temple murals.
